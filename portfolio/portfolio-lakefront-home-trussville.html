Lake Front Home – Black Farmhouse

A ground-up lakefront residence shaped to the shoreline—capturing morning light, framed views, and easy indoor–outdoor living for a family that loves the water.

Location Trussville, Alabama
Lakefront Custom HomeIndoor–Outdoor LivingEnergy-Efficient Envelope

Project Overview

A lakefront home shaped by light, structure, and connection.

This custom lakefront home in Trussville, Alabama pairs vaulted gathering spaces with expansive glazing to keep the water always in view. The structure steps with the hillside, creating a bright walk-out level below and a main floor that lives to the deck.

Natural materials—warm woods, stone, and durable exterior finishes—ground the home in its setting. Large openings slide away to blend kitchen, great room, and terraces for effortless hosting.

Home Highlights

  • Vaulted great room aligned to long lake views
  • Entertainer’s kitchen with scullery and deck connection
  • Owner’s suite with private terrace and spa-inspired bath
  • Window-lined stair hall bringing daylight to the core
  • Walk-out lower level with outdoor kitchen, fire feature, and dock access

Signature Details

  • Engineered roof trusses shaping open, vaulted interiors
  • Moisture management waterproofed foundation, perimeter drainage
  • High-performance glazing with integrated shading for comfort
  • Durable, lakeside-ready exterior materials and trim
  • Energy-efficient systems tuned for humidity and seasonal swings

Trussville Custom Home Builder

Designing lakefront homes that embrace the shoreline.

Riveroak Building Company is a Trussville custom home builder focused on waterfront residences that balance engineering, craftsmanship, and lifestyle. From evaluating steep lots to coordinating boat docks and outdoor kitchens, our team plans every detail so your home performs beautifully in Alabama’s lakeside climate.

For this Black Farmhouse, we partnered with the homeowners early to optimize the floor plan for the water views and to integrate service-area permitting requirements. We also helped the family prepare for future smart-home upgrades and energy monitoring, ensuring the residence will adapt alongside them.

Site-sensitive planning

We analyzed the hillside terrain, soil conditions, and prevailing winds before breaking ground, positioning the home to take advantage of sunrise views while protecting outdoor rooms from summer heat.

Waterfront lifestyle amenities

Entertaining zones extend from the main kitchen to multiple decks, a screened porch, and a lower-level lounge steps from the dock—ideal for family gatherings and weekend guests.

Construction expertise

Our craftsmen engineered the roof trusses for the dramatic vaulted ceilings and installed moisture-management systems that keep the structure protected through every season on the lake.

Material Story

A palette grounded in the lakeside landscape.

Each finish was selected to echo the wooded shoreline — honest materials that balance strength, texture, and timeless simplicity.

Wood framing and sheathing

Brick and board-and-batten siding
