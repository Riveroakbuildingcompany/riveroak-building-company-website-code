Modern Farmhouse – Trussville

A timeless Trussville farmhouse that pairs welcoming porches and custom millwork with light-filled, modern interiors for everyday family living.

Location Trussville, Alabama
Modern FarmhouseFamily Gathering SpacesCustom Millwork

Project Overview

Bright, inviting, and built to feel like home from day one.

This modern farmhouse in Trussville blends traditional charm and clean, current design. Riveroak worked closely with the homeowners to shape a home that feels both open and grounded — as beautiful as it is functional.

From the wide front porch and board-and-batten exterior to black-framed windows and custom millwork inside, every detail was chosen for warmth, comfort, and longevity. The result honors farmhouse tradition while embracing modern Southern living.

Home Highlights

  • Welcoming front porch with full-length roofline
  • Open kitchen, dining, and living area centered around natural light
  • Owner’s suite with vaulted ceilings and spa-like bath
  • Upstairs loft connecting guest and children’s rooms
  • Rear patio and sloped lot with landscaped views

Signature Details

  • Board-and-batten siding paired with brick accents
  • Custom black windows with divided-light patterns
  • Handmade cabinetry and a large central kitchen island
  • Warm wood floors and modern lantern lighting
  • High-efficiency design for year-round comfort and light

Building the Modern Farmhouse – Trussville

Material Story

A palette that balances warmth and clean lines.

Classic farmhouse form meets modern detailing—each finish selected to bring light, contrast, and timeless durability to this Trussville home.

Board-and-batten siding

White fiber-cement siding and vertical battens define the farmhouse silhouette, pairing crisp geometry with lasting performance.

Brick porch foundations

Hand-laid brick bases ground the wide front porch and add warmth against the home’s light exterior tones.

Metal and shingle roofing

Charcoal architectural shingles with metal accent roofing blend texture and strength for all-season protection.

Interior craftsmanship

Granite counters, dark cabinetry, and warm oak floors create balance—modern edges softened by natural materials and light.
