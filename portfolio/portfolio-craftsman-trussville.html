Project Overview

Craftsman character, built for lasting family roots.

This custom Craftsman home in Trussville, Alabama is taking shape on a wooded lot surrounded by tall pines — a peaceful setting that inspired its design. Built by Riveroak Building Company, the home blends traditional proportions with modern construction methods, using a strong wood frame, dimensional roofing, and a weather-protected envelope to ensure decades of durability.

Once complete, the residence will feature warm exterior materials, detailed trimwork, and a layout tailored to everyday family life — open gathering spaces, quiet bedrooms, and light-filled transitions that bring the outdoors in. Every board, beam, and finish reflects our commitment to building homes with soul and staying power.

Home Highlights

  • Set on a wooded homesite in Trussville, Alabama
  • Architectural rooflines and classic Craftsman gables
  • Open-concept kitchen and living area designed for gathering
  • Owner’s suite positioned for privacy and wooded views
  • Covered outdoor living spaces extending from the main level

Signature Details

  • Engineered wood framing and HomeGuard weather barrier
  • Wide eaves, layered trim, and exposed rafter detailing
  • Brick foundation accents and durable exterior siding
  • Natural finishes and handcrafted millwork inside
  • Energy-efficient systems built for long-term comfort
