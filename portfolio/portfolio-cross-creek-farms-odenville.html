Community Overview

A neighborhood for custom living in Odenville.

Cross Creek Farms is a Riveroak-supported community where buyers can shape a new home to fit their lifestyle. Just minutes from Odenville conveniences, the neighborhood features mature trees, rolling terrain, and modern utilities — an ideal canvas for basement homes and multi-level living.

Homeowners can bring their own plan or collaborate with our team to design something unique. Each lot offers flexible footprints, allowing families to maximize natural light, outdoor living, and future expansion.

Community Snapshot

  • 47 total homesites with underground utilities
  • Basement-friendly grades across multiple cul-de-sacs
  • Convenient access to Odenville schools, shopping, and I-59
  • Protective covenants that maintain neighborhood value
  • Scenic views framed by established hardwoods

Build Opportunities

  • Bring your own architectural plans or design with Riveroak
  • Customizable elevations, outdoor living, and interior finishes
  • Space for multi-car garages, workshops, or in-law suites
  • Energy-efficient construction tailored to the homesite
  • On-site guidance from Riveroak throughout design and build
