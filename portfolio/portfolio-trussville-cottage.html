English Cottage

Compact in footprint yet generous in character, this cottage brings light, warmth, and efficient living to a wooded Trussville lot.

Location Trussville, Alabama
Cottage ArchitectureLight-Filled LivingEfficient Footprint

Project Overview

Compact in size—generous in heart and light.

Tucked into a wooded lot in Trussville, this cottage was designed for a young family that wanted efficiency without compromise. Riveroak shaped a simple, smart plan beneath steep gables and dormers, pairing durable construction with warm, livable spaces.

Vaulted ceilings and thoughtfully placed windows pull natural light deep into the home, while tailored trim and durable finishes keep maintenance easy. Every square foot works hard—open where it counts, quiet where it matters.

Trussville custom home builders bringing English cottage living to life

This English cottage was crafted by Riveroak Building Company, a Trussville-area custom home builder focused on livable luxury and lasting value. The plan balances a compact footprint with airy rooms, giving Birmingham families the character they love without sacrificing everyday convenience.

Material Story

Crafted for comfort and timeless charm.

Rooted in traditional cottage design, each material reflects the home's focus on warmth, longevity, and handcrafted detail.

Concrete block foundation

Engineered to fit the wooded slope, the foundation anchors the structure and provides lasting stability.

Wood framing and roofline

Exposed timber framing defines the vaulted interiors while durable asphalt shingles shelter the home’s steep gables.

Weather barrier and energy windows

High-performance HomeGuard wrap and energy-efficient windows protect against the elements and brighten every room.

Tile and trim finishes

Porcelain marble-look tile, mosaic shower flooring, and custom millwork add everyday luxury and cottage character throughout.
