Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

Riveroak Building Company is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We continually work to improve the user experience for everyone who visits riveroakbuilding.com and apply the relevant accessibility standards whenever feasible for our Birmingham, Alabama clients.

1. Accessibility Standards

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA standards whenever possible. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user friendly for everyone.

2. Measures We’ve Taken

To support accessibility, our team has implemented the following features and best practices:

  • Semantic HTML structure to help assistive technologies understand page content.
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation and interactive elements with clear focus states.
  • Text alternatives for non-text content such as images and icons.
  • Responsive layouts that adapt to different devices, orientations, and zoom levels.
  • Color contrast and typography choices designed to improve readability.

3. Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review new features, content, and third-party integrations to identify opportunities for improvement. As industry standards evolve, we will continue to adjust our design and development practices to maintain and enhance accessibility.

4. Supported Browsers & Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with the most recent versions of major browsers, including Chrome, Safari, Edge, and Firefox. We also strive to support commonly used assistive technologies such as screen readers, screen magnifiers, and voice recognition software.

5. Known Limitations

While we make every effort to ensure accessibility, some legacy content or third-party applications may not fully meet our standards. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please let us know so we can address them promptly.

6. Accessibility Support for Birmingham Homeowners

Riveroak Building Company serves discerning homeowners throughout Birmingham, Mountain Brook, and Vestavia Hills. If you need assistance navigating our accessibility features or require project details in an alternative format, we are ready to help. Our team can coordinate large-print materials, phone consultations, and on-site meetings to ensure every stage of your luxury home build remains inclusive.
