About Carrington Lakes

Lakeside luxury with a nature-forward point of view.

Carrington Lakes is a master-planned, gated community in Trussville spanning roughly 1,500 acres of rolling hills, mature pines, and private lakes. With six stocked lakes, community boat launches, and miles of hiking and biking trails, homeowners enjoy a resort-like lifestyle grounded in the outdoors.

As a trusted Carrington Lakes custom home builder, Riveroak designs dwellings that frame the views, maximize natural light, and extend living spaces to porches, docks, and outdoor rooms. Every project is tailored to the architectural guidelines and amenities that make the neighborhood feel cohesive and elevated.

From waterfront homesites to wooded retreats near the nature preserve, we curate every decision—architecture, materials, landscape integration—so your property lives beautifully from sunrise paddle sessions to evening gatherings.

What to Expect When Building with Riveroak

  • Personal collaboration from design concept to final punch list.
  • Site-sensitive planning that embraces lake views, topography, and existing tree canopy.
  • Curated finishes, millwork, and outdoor amenities selected for longevity in a lakeside environment.
  • Transparent budgets and schedules aligned with HOA milestones and community standards.

Local Insights

Building in harmony with Carrington Lakes’ landscape and lifestyle.

Carrington Lakes is one of Trussville’s premier gated communities, known for its rolling hills, five stocked lakes, and resort-style amenities. Riveroak works closely with the architectural review board and HOA design guidelines to ensure each home reflects both your personal vision and the neighborhood’s timeless aesthetic.

Every build begins with a deep understanding of the site—from lakefront orientation and drainage patterns to natural stone, brick, and color palette requirements. Our designs emphasize lake views, seamless indoor-outdoor transitions, and a natural flow that complements the surrounding preserve.

Whether it’s a new custom home, a renovation of a legacy property, or an outdoor living addition, we manage permitting, inspections, and HOA coordination with precision. Expect proactive communication, on-site walkthroughs, and Riveroak’s signature commitment to craftsmanship and clarity.

With miles of walking trails, tennis courts, and private lakes, Carrington Lakes offers a peaceful retreat within minutes of downtown Trussville. Riveroak builds homes that feel equally at home in this community—rooted in nature, built for connection, and designed to last for generations.

FAQ

Carrington Lakes building questions—answered.

What impacts the cost of a custom home in Carrington Lakes?

Pricing is shaped by homesite location (lakefront vs. wooded interior lots), topography and site prep, and the level of finishes you select—from window walls and natural stone to custom outdoor amenities. After an on-site meeting, Riveroak prepares a detailed estimate that reflects Carrington Lakes’ requirements and your design vision.

Do you coordinate with the Carrington Lakes HOA?

Yes. We manage architectural review submissions, HOA approvals, and any required revisions. Our team is experienced with Carrington Lakes’ guidelines for exterior materials, landscaping, and amenities, keeping your project compliant and on schedule.

Can you help maximize indoor-outdoor living?

Absolutely. We design screened porches, sun decks, boat launches, and outdoor kitchens that align with community standards while making the most of the lakes and nature preserve. Every detail—from drainage to lighting—is engineered for durability and year-round enjoyment.

Do you renovate existing Carrington Lakes homes?

Yes. Riveroak completes whole-home renovations, additions, and interior refreshes for established residences. We modernize layouts, mechanical systems, and finishes while keeping the architecture cohesive with the community and surrounding landscape.
