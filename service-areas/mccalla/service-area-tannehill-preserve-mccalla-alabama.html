FAQ

Tannehill Preserve home building questions—answered.

How does Riveroak plan homes for Tannehill Preserve’s protected greenspace?

We study the land first—evaluating grade changes, pond sites, trail proximity, and tree canopies. That informs driveway routing, foundation design, and outdoor living placement so your home feels integrated with the preserve from the start.

Can Riveroak coordinate approvals with the Tannehill Preserve HOA and local agencies?

Yes. We collaborate with HOA architectural committees, Jefferson County officials, utilities, and adjacent municipalities to secure approvals and inspections, keeping paperwork and timelines organized on your behalf.

Do you design amenity structures like carriage houses or outdoor pavilions?

Absolutely. We plan carriage houses, workshops, pool houses, and outdoor pavilions that match your primary residence and make resort-style living effortless. Our team oversees utilities, grading, and finishes for a cohesive estate.

How will Riveroak keep us informed if we live outside the preserve?

Expect weekly updates, virtual walkthroughs, and digital selections boards. Whether you are local or traveling, you have direct access to Riveroak leadership for real-time decisions and transparent budget tracking.
