About Navajo Hills

Craft a custom home within Alabaster’s character-rich enclave.

Set within Alabaster, Alabama (35007), Navajo Hills balances privacy and convenience: mature tree canopies, generous setbacks, and quick routes to US-31 and I-65. Its gently rolling terrain and established streetscape invite site-responsive architecture—homes that feel rooted in place while opening naturally to outdoor living.

With Riveroak, you’ll translate that setting into a tailored residence: graceful elevations that respect the neighborhood’s rhythm, modern performance and energy efficiency, and interiors planned for everyday ease—light, storage, circulation, and indoor-outdoor flow.

Whether a preservation-minded renovation or a ground-up estate, our team coordinates architectural design, structural engineering, and construction sequencing with transparent communication and detailed budgeting. The result is a home that honors Navajo Hills’ character and serves your family for decades.

FAQ

Navajo Hills home building questions—answered.

What makes Navajo Hills ideal for a custom home?

Navajo Hills offers one of the most naturally beautiful settings in Alabaster, Alabama (35007) — spacious lots, mature hardwoods, and gentle elevation changes that allow for creative design. These qualities give homeowners the flexibility to design a custom home that blends privacy, curb appeal, and meaningful indoor-outdoor connections. Riveroak’s approach emphasizes architecture that enhances the landscape, with floorplans that flow seamlessly from entry to patio to wooded views.

Can Riveroak renovate or expand an existing Navajo Hills home?

Absolutely. Many homeowners in Navajo Hills choose to stay rooted in the neighborhood while updating their spaces. We specialize in whole-home renovations, additions, and kitchen or bath reconfigurations that preserve architectural character while elevating comfort and functionality. From reimagining a 1980s floorplan to adding a detached garage or guest suite, our design-build team ensures the new and existing flow as one cohesive home.

Do you handle permits and neighborhood approvals for Navajo Hills projects?

Yes — Riveroak manages every phase of permitting, from Shelby County applications and utility coordination to final inspections and HOA architectural reviews (if applicable). Our experience with local codes and subdivision requirements allows us to anticipate documentation needs early, keeping your project on schedule and compliant from day one.

How does Riveroak approach site planning for Navajo Hills’ trees and slopes?

Every property in Navajo Hills deserves a design that works with, not against, its natural terrain. Our preconstruction process includes grading analysis, drainage mapping, and arborist collaboration to protect mature trees and ensure proper water flow. Whether building a new foundation or adding outdoor amenities, we prioritize long-term stability and aesthetics — ensuring your home stands gracefully within its wooded surroundings.

Can Riveroak help design outdoor living spaces in Navajo Hills?

Yes — outdoor living is a defining feature of life in Navajo Hills. We design and build screened porches, patios, outdoor kitchens, and terraced gardens that reflect each property’s natural elevation and privacy. These spaces become natural extensions of your home, connecting daily life with the beauty of Alabaster’s landscape.
