About the Neighborhood

Modern neighborhood comforts with a tranquil Moody backdrop.

The Reserve at the Highlands is a thoughtfully planned community framed by sidewalk-lined streets, manicured green spaces, and scenic, gently rolling terrain. Moments from Moody’s award-winning schools and local shopping, it delivers a peaceful, family-friendly setting without sacrificing convenience to Birmingham.

Riveroak Building Company brings concierge-level service to every homesite. From early feasibility and HOA coordination through finish selections and post-build care, we align architecture, engineering, and construction to honor the character of your Reserve property.

We collaborate closely with St. Clair County officials, utility partners, and the community’s architectural review committee, ensuring your home reflects Riveroak craftsmanship while staying fully compliant and on schedule.

FAQ

The Reserve at the Highlands home building questions—answered.

What influences the cost of a custom home in The Reserve at the Highlands?

The cost to build within The Reserve at the Highlands (35004) depends on your lot’s slope, desired outdoor amenities, utility upgrades, and the level of custom detail and finishes you choose. Following an on-site consultation and design discussion, we prepare a comprehensive estimate outlining site work, architectural scope, and selections so you can make confident decisions.

Do you manage HOA approvals and city permits for Reserve homes?

Yes. Our team manages City of Moody permits, St. Clair County inspections, and all Reserve architectural review submissions. We coordinate drawings, specifications, and timelines so approvals are handled efficiently and your project stays on track from start to finish.

Can you help prepare my Reserve lot before construction?

Absolutely. Before breaking ground, we assess drainage, soil, and grading conditions to shape a stable foundation and efficient layout. From stormwater management to driveway access planning, we work with surveyors and engineers to ensure your homesite is construction-ready.

Do you renovate or expand existing homes in The Reserve?

Yes—we complete whole-home renovations, additions, and outdoor living spaces for Reserve homeowners looking to enhance their property. From kitchen and bath updates to bonus suites, detached garages, and back porch extensions, we deliver seamless upgrades that feel original to your home’s design.
