About Hollywood

Preserving Homewood’s hillside icon.

Tucked along Shades Creek Parkway and framed by rolling hills, Hollywood in Homewood, Alabama (35209) stands as one of Birmingham’s most architecturally distinctive neighborhoods. Recognized for its Spanish and Mediterranean Revival homes with red-clay tile roofs, archways, and stucco facades, Hollywood’s charm lies in its timeless craftsmanship and lush, tree-lined streets. Listed on the National Register of Historic Places, this enclave embodies old-world elegance balanced with proximity to modern convenience — moments from SoHo Square, Samford University, and downtown Birmingham.

Riveroak Building Company serves as a trusted partner for homeowners seeking to enhance or reimagine these celebrated properties. As an experienced Homewood custom home builder, we navigate historic preservation guidelines, steep elevations, and tight lot constraints with precision and care. From ground-up construction to whole-home restorations, we choreograph every stage — zoning, permitting, structural planning, and fine detailing — to create homes that feel both authentically Hollywood and undeniably yours.

Every Riveroak project blends luxury design, heritage respect, and structural integrity. We coordinate with local architects, artisans, and inspectors to ensure each residence reflects the neighborhood’s Mediterranean soul while performing beautifully for modern living. Whether restoring a 1920s estate or introducing a new hillside retreat, our work honors Hollywood’s legacy — one detail, one arch, one handcrafted finish at a time.

Local Insights

Hollywood living with effortless access.

Nestled within the 35209 ZIP code, Hollywood in Homewood, Alabama blends hillside views, Mediterranean architecture, and a sense of timeless refinement just minutes from Downtown Birmingham. Originally developed in the 1920s as a Spanish-inspired retreat, this historic neighborhood sits gracefully between Mountain Brook and Shades Creek, offering proximity to SoHo Square, Brookwood Village, and the scenic Shades Creek Greenway. Clay-tiled roofs and arched porticos line Hollywood Boulevard, while winding, tree-canopied side streets reveal cottages and villas that showcase the area’s cinematic charm.

Riveroak Building Company approaches every Hollywood project with the respect this district deserves. We coordinate construction logistics for narrow hillside drives, limited parking, and mature landscaping—maintaining the tranquility and elegance residents cherish. Our team works hand-in-hand with Homewood City Hall, Jefferson County inspectors, and neighborhood associations to navigate permitting, stormwater guidelines, and architectural reviews with ease.

From artisan plasterwork and hand-forged iron railings to custom hardwood cabinetry and terracotta finishes, Riveroak curates materials that complement Hollywood’s architectural narrative while enhancing daily life. The result is a home that feels authentic, enduring, and unmistakably rooted in this iconic Homewood enclave.

FAQ

Hollywood home building questions—answered.

How much does it cost to renovate or build in Hollywood, Homewood?

Homebuilding and renovation costs in Hollywood, Homewood (35209) depend on factors like lot elevation, architectural detailing, and preservation requirements. Historic stucco and tile homes often involve specialized materials and artisanship to ensure period accuracy. After your on-site consultation, Riveroak Building Company provides a detailed proposal outlining investment ranges, allowances, milestone phases, and finish options—so you can plan with clarity and confidence from the very beginning.

Can Riveroak work within Hollywood’s historic preservation guidelines?

Yes. Our team regularly collaborates with Homewood’s Historic Preservation Commission and neighborhood design review boards. We prepare all required architectural documentation, coordinate submissions, and oversee approvals to ensure compliance with Hollywood’s Spanish Revival and Mediterranean style standards. From wrought iron balconies to arched entries and clay-tile roofs, every Riveroak project is designed to preserve the neighborhood’s historic identity while integrating today’s building performance and energy efficiency standards.

Do you handle landscape design and outdoor living enhancements?

Absolutely. Hollywood’s hillside lots and mature landscapes call for thoughtful outdoor planning. Riveroak designs and constructs outdoor kitchens, pools, courtyards, and terraces that complement each home’s architecture and natural surroundings. We manage everything from drainage and grading to lighting and hardscaping, ensuring that your exterior spaces extend seamlessly from your interiors—ideal for entertaining, relaxing, and enjoying Birmingham’s year-round climate.

Do you work with local architects and artisans?

Yes. Riveroak Building Company partners with Birmingham and Homewood’s leading architects, craftsmen, and preservation specialists who understand the neighborhood’s architectural DNA. Our network includes plaster artisans, tile restorers, and ironworkers with experience in historic homebuilding. This collaboration ensures that every home—whether a new Mediterranean residence or a Hollywood historic renovation—is authentic, enduring, and built to the highest standards.

What makes Riveroak different from other Birmingham builders?

Riveroak is more than a builder—we’re stewards of Hollywood’s architectural legacy. Our design–build approach integrates planning, construction, and communication into a unified experience. We provide concierge-level project management, transparency in budgeting, and genuine collaboration at every milestone. As a locally trusted firm, we understand Hollywood’s terrain, permitting process, and design expectations—allowing us to deliver homes that are both architecturally faithful and functionally modern.
